About Us

These are Ricard and Laura, they met in college, and even though they eventually studied different subjects, they continued on the same path together. Ricard focused on machine learning and image processing. In contrast, Laura decided to study languages and how to teach them. A year ago, while walking in the forests of Girona and Mannheim, they began to think about how to help people with their land and forest management. Why not know how many trees you have and what type they are on your property? They got the project off the ground to help farmers and individuals monitor their plots.

Ricard Durall holds a Ph.D. in computer vision, and he is passionate about using AI technology to solve real-world issues.

Laura Montilla holds a M.A. in audiovisual translation, and she is a linguist and an enthusiastic communicator.
